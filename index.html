About

Ceetoo foods and drinks was born out of a passion for bringing people together through the art of food and drink. Established by a group of foodies, our journey began with a dream to create a space where flavors could evoke memories, and where every meal could be a celebration of both tradition and innovation.


Our mission is simple yet profound: to offer an exceptional dining experience that fuses the time-honored traditions of Nepali cuisine with modern culinary techniques. We are committed to providing a warm and welcoming ambiance, where each dish tells a story and every sip of our carefully crafted drinks is an adventure.
